About us

Earth 2 You is looking to build relationships and opportunities in the growing intersection of applied climate data science between academia, government labs and the private sector. Supporting new climate science insights with a business-minded focus and start-up urgency.

The rapid changes we are observing in our climate system motivate us to find, or indeed carve, a new path forward to better extract information hidden within the growing collection of remote sensing and climate model assets we have at our disposal.

There is a growing number of companies in need of climate data expertise and a growing number of climate scientists looking for new career development opportunities.
